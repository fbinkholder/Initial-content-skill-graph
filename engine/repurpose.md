# Repurposing Chain

How one idea becomes seven pieces of platform-optimized content.

The goal is NOT reformatting. The goal is platform-native adaptation that respects each platform's culture while maintaining brand voice.

---

## The Chain

```
TikTok (write first)
    ↓
Instagram (polish, carousel-ify)
    ↓
YouTube (expand for depth, extract Shorts)
    ↓
X (sharpen to punches)
    ↓
Facebook (warm up, add questions)
    ↓
Newsletter (go deep, add vulnerability)
    ↓
Pinterest (most polished, most evergreen)
```

---

## Step-by-Step Adaptation

### Step 1: TikTok (Origin)

Write the raw idea here first. This is the discovery engine.

**What you create:**
- 45-90 second video script
- Spoken, not written
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- One clear idea, one clear takeaway

**Example:**
```
Hook: "okay so 67% of people can't find their foundation shade and we're just... accepting that?"

Content: "I worked the Sephora floor for years. every single day I'd watch people try shade after shade and leave empty-handed. 40 shades per brand and still nothing. the problem was never the number of options. the problem was the manufacturing constraint that limits how shades get made in the first place..."

CTA: "we're building something that changes this. link in bio."
```

---

### Step 2: Instagram (Polish)

Take the TikTok idea and give it visual sophistication.

**Adaptation choices:**
- **Reel:** Slightly more polished edit of TikTok video
- **Carousel:** Transform the script into 7-10 visual slides
- **Single image:** Key stat or quote as branded visual

**Carousel transformation:**
```
Slide 1: "67% of people can't find their shade"
Slide 2: "The problem isn't you"
Slide 3: "It's how shades are manufactured"
Slide 4: "40 shades sounds like variety"
Slide 5: "But it's actually limitation dressed up as choice"
Slide 6: "What if precision matching was possible?"
Slide 7: "4.5 million shades. 9 refillable pods"
Slide 8: "Join the waitlist @chromarabeauty"
```

**Caption:** Two paragraphs. Hook/teach, then deepen + CTA.

---

### Step 3: YouTube (Expand)

Take the core idea and build depth around it.

**Adaptation choices:**
- **Short:** Extract best 30-60 seconds from TikTok
- **Long-form:** Expand the idea into 8-15 minute educational video

**Long-form expansion:**
```
Title: "Why 67% of People Can't Find Their Foundation Shade (The Real Reason)"

0:00 - Hook: The stat that should make you angry
0:30 - The visible problem (returns, waste, frustration)
2:00 - The hidden cause (manufacturing constraints)
5:00 - Why 40 shades isn't actually variety
7:00 - What precision matching actually means
9:00 - How NovaMirror works (outputs only, no mechanism details)
11:00 - What this changes for you
12:00 - Waitlist CTA
```

### Step 4: X (Sharpen)

Distill to the sharpest possible version. Every word must earn its place.

**Adaptation choices:**
- **Single tweet:** One punchy line
- **Thread:** 3-10 tweets breaking down the idea

**Single tweet versions:**
```
"67% can't find their shade. 40% of foundation gets returned. $2B+ in waste yearly. and we keep launching more 40-shade ranges"
```

```
"the beauty industry invented 40 shades, watched 67% of people still fail to find a match, and then blamed undertones"
```

**Thread version:**
```
Tweet 1: "why 67% of people can't find their foundation shade 🧵"
Tweet 2: "it's not because they don't know their undertone"

```

## Content Pillars

Every idea should map to one of these pillars:

| Pillar | Focus | Emotional Core |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| The Problem | Shade matching failure, industry critique | Frustration, validation |
| The Solution | NovaMirror, precision matching, 4.5M shades | Hope, excitement |
| The Why | Founder story, Sephora floor experience | Trust, authenticity |
| The Industry | Sustainability, manufacturing constraints, returns | Education, insight |
| The Community | Shared experiences, waitlist, movement | Belonging, momentum |

---

## Repurposing Checklist

Before publishing adapted content, verify:

- [ ] Tone matches platform (see [[platform-tone]])
- [ ] Format is platform-native (not just resized)
- [ ] Hook is adapted for platform culture
- [ ] Voice remains consistent despite tone shift
- [ ] Would someone following all platforms be annoyed seeing "the same thing"? (If yes, adapt more)

---

## Batch Workflow

For efficiency, create all 7 versions in one session:

1. Write TikTok script first
2. Immediately adapt to all other platforms
3. Review all versions together before scheduling
4. Schedule forward using [[scheduling]] cadence

This maintains consistency and catches drift between versions.

---

## The Litmus Test

After adapting, ask: "Would someone following Chromara on all 7 platforms feel like they're seeing repetitive content, or feel like each platform offers something different?"

If the answer is "repetitive," the adaptation isn't deep enough.

Same core message. Different execution. Every time.
