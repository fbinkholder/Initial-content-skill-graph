## Batch Production Workflow

### Weekly Batch Session

**When:** Sunday or Monday
**Duration:** 4-6 hours
**Output:** All content for the week across all platforms

**Process:**
1. **Ideation (30 min):** Generate 3-5 core ideas for the week
2. **TikTok first (60 min):** Write all TikTok scripts
3. **Repurpose chain (90 min):** Adapt each idea to all 7 platforms per [[repurpose]]
4. **Visual production (60 min):** Create carousels, pin graphics, thumbnails
5. **Review (30 min):** Quality check all content together
6. **Schedule (30 min):** Load into scheduling tools

### Daily Check-In

**When:** 15 minutes, mid-day
**Tasks:**
- Check engagement on recent posts
- Respond to comments/DMs
- Note what's performing for [[winners]]/[[losers]]
- Post Stories (real-time, not batched)

### Friday Review

**When:** 30 minutes, end of week
**Tasks:**
- Review week's performance metrics
- Update [[winners]] and [[losers]]
- Note any pattern changes for [[changelog]]
- Prep ideas for next week's batch

## Holiday/Event Considerations

Avoid major scheduling during:
- Major US holidays (lower engagement)
- Major beauty industry events (noise competition)
- Breaking news days (algorithm deprioritizes brand content)

Lean into:
- Beauty-relevant awareness days
- Industry news/controversy (commentary opportunity)
- Accelerator announcements (if relevant)

---

## Consistency Rules

1. **Never go dark.** Even one day of silence breaks momentum
2. **TikTok is non-negotiable.** This is the discovery engine
3. **Stories are daily.** Even if just one quick behind-scenes
4. **Newsletter is sacred.** Subscribers expect it weekly
5. **Pinterest compounds.** Consistent pinning pays off over months
