# TikTok

Platform-specific rules for TikTok content.
See [[brand-voice]] for core voice. See [[platform-tone]] for tone adaptation.

---

## Platform Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Priority | Highest. Write TikTok FIRST in content chain |
| Algorithm | Discovery-based. Content shown to non-followers |
| Consumption | Sound-off majority. Text overlays mandatory |
| Aesthetic | Raw over polished. Authenticity wins |
| Posting frequency | 2x/day minimum, 14+/week |

---

## Content Specifications

**Length:** 45-90 seconds (sweet spot: 60 sec)
**Hook window:** 1-2 seconds maximum
**Format:** Vertical 9:16
**Captions:** Always on. Muted viewing is default

---

## What Works

- Raw, unfiltered founder energy
- Fast-paced education with visual proof
- Relatable frustration about shade matching
- "Wait, this is actually insane" moments
- Behind-the-scenes of building Chromara
- Specific pain points that feel personal
- Trend sounds when authentically applicable

---

## What Fails

- Polished brand voice
- Slow buildups
- Dense information without visual support
- Corporate tone
- Generic "inclusive beauty" language
- Scripted-sounding delivery

---

## Hook Patterns (TikTok-Optimized)

From [[hooks]], these work best here:

- **Shared Frustration:** "If you've ever bought two foundations just to mix them together, this is for you"
- **Sharp Critique:** "The beauty industry loves talking about recycling and hates talking about wrong shade waste"
- **Observation:** "Ever notice how 'close enough' became the standard for foundation matching?"
- **Question:** "Why are we still expected to become chemists just to make foundation work?"
- **Specific Pain Point:** "If your South Asian skin turns orange two hours after applying foundation, this is for you"

---

## Tone on TikTok

Most energetic version of the voice. Spoken, not written.

**Allowed:**
- "okay so" "literally" "like" "wait" as conversational markers
- Blunt frustration about industry BS
- Visible emotion and passion
- Casual profanity when authentic

**Not allowed:**
- Corporate smoothness
- Hedging ("might," "could potentially")
- Banned phrases from [[brand-voice]]

---

## Script Structure

```
[0-2 sec] HOOK — grab immediately, text overlay
[2-45 sec] CONTENT — deliver value, show don't tell
[45-60 sec] CTA — soft close, waitlist if natural
```

---

## Example Script

```
HOOK: "okay so you're telling me 67% of people can't find foundation that matches and we're just... accepting that?"

CONTENT: "I worked the Sephora floor for years. every single day I'd watch people try shade after shade after shade and leave empty-handed because nothing worked. 40 shades per brand and still nothing. the problem was never the number of options. the problem was the manufacturing constraint..."

CTA: "we're building something that changes this. link in bio if you want to be first in line."
```

## Campaign Mode ("Donate $1")

During campaign: 3-4 posts/day across Brand + Personal accounts

Content mix:
- 40% problem education
- 30% solution preview
- 20% founder story
- 10% community/social proof
