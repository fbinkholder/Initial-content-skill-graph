# Instagram

Platform-specific rules for Instagram content.
See [[brand-voice]] for core voice. See [[platform-tone]] for tone adaptation.

---

## Platform Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Priority | Second. Adapt from TikTok, polish for visual quality |
| Algorithm | Follower-weighted + discovery through Reels/Explore |
| Consumption | Visual-first. Aesthetic quality matters |
| Aesthetic | Most polished version of content |
| Posting frequency | 1x/day feed + daily Stories |

---

## Content Types

### Feed Posts
- **Carousels:** 7-10 slides. Highest engagement format
- **Reels:** Adapted from TikTok, slightly more polished
- **Single images:** Brand visuals, announcements

### Stories
- Daily presence
- Behind-the-scenes
- Polls, questions, engagement stickers
- Drive to feed posts and link

---

## Carousel Specifications

**Slide count:** 7-10 slides
**Slide 1:** Bold hook, max 7-10 words
**Slides 2-8:** One idea per slide, max 30 words
**Final slide:** CTA with @chromarabeauty

**Design requirements:**
- 1:1 or 4:5 aspect ratio
- Brand colors: Violet #8C52FF, Near-Black #0D0D0F, Chrome Silver #E5E7EB
- Typography: Montserrat bold for headlines
- Readable at thumbnail size
- Consistent style across all slides

---

## Caption Structure

```
[Paragraph 1] Hook, teach, or reframe. Deliver value immediately

[Paragraph 2] Deepen the point, add context, end with benefit-led CTA

Join the waitlist through the link in bio.
```

**Hashtags:** 3-5 strategic hashtags only. No spam.

---

## What Works

- Sharp visual-text combos
- Myth vs truth carousels
- Consumer behavior insights
- Editorial observations
- Educational Reels
- Before/after frameworks
- Specific pain point validation

---

## What Fails

- Generic brand captions
- Text that doesn't match visual quality
- Dense paragraphs
- Hashtag spam
- Salesy CTAs
- Low-quality visuals
- Reposting TikTok without adaptation

---

## Tone on Instagram

Polished but warm. Elevated casual.

**Energy:** Smart friend at a nice dinner, not boardroom presentation

**Allowed:**
- Conversational but never sloppy
- Deeper education than TikTok
- Aspirational energy
- Clean, considered language

**Not allowed:**
- Raw/heated energy (save for TikTok)
- Sloppy casual
- Corporate stiffness
- Banned phrases from [[brand-voice]]
