# YouTube

Platform-specific rules for YouTube content.
See [[brand-voice]] for core voice. See [[platform-tone]] for tone adaptation.

---

## Platform Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Priority | Third. SEO-driven, evergreen value |
| Algorithm | Search + suggested. Title is everything |
| Consumption | Intent-driven. Viewers come looking for answers |
| Aesthetic | Higher production than TikTok, still authentic |
| Posting frequency | 2x/week long-form + 3-5 Shorts/week |

---

## Content Types

### Long-Form (8-15 minutes)
- Deep tutorials
- Founder story
- Educational explainers
- System walkthroughs
- Industry analysis

### Shorts (30-60 seconds)
- Extracted from long-form best moments
- Adapted from TikTok/Reels
- Quick tips and insights

---

## Long-Form Structure

```
[0:00-0:30] HOOK — why this matters, what they'll learn
[0:30-2:00] CONTEXT — the problem, why it exists
[2:00-10:00] MAIN CONTENT — chapters for each section
[10:00-12:00] RECAP + CTA — summary, waitlist
```

**Requirements:**
- SEO-optimized title
- Hook in first 5 seconds
- Chapter timestamps in description
- Thumbnail with readable text
- B-roll and visual variety

---

## Title Formulas

- "Why [X]% of People [Problem] (The Real Reason)"
- "I [Worked/Spent] [Time] [Doing X]. Here's What I Learned"
- "What Nobody Tells You About [Topic]"
- "[Number] Things [Audience] Gets Wrong About [Topic]"

**Examples:**
- "Why 67% of People Can't Find Their Foundation Shade (The Real Reason)"
- "I Worked the Sephora Floor for 6 Years. Here's What I Learned About Shade Matching"
- "What Nobody Tells You About Foundation Returns"

---

## Shorts Specifications

**Length:** 30-60 seconds
**Format:** Vertical 9:16
**Hook:** First 2 seconds
**Subtitles:** Always on

**Sources:**
- Best 30-60 sec segment from long-form
- Adapted from high-performing TikTok
- Original quick-hit content

---

## What Works

- Strong SEO titles
- Clear chapter structure
- Educational depth with proof
- Founder authenticity
- B-roll of product/prototype
- Before/after demonstrations
- Answering search queries directly

---

## What Fails

- Slow intros
- Burying the hook
- Generic titles
- Talking head without visual variety
- No clear takeaway
- Clickbait that doesn't deliver
- Over-produced, inauthentic feel

---

## Tone on YouTube

Most structured and educational. Authority mode with warmth.

**Energy:** Expert teaching a class, but still human

**Allowed:**
- Longer, more detailed explanations
- Step-by-step depth
- Authority positioning
- Casual moments within structure

**Not allowed:**
- Dry, lecture-style delivery
- Corporate tone
- Banned phrases from [[brand-voice]]


## Posting Schedule

| Day | Content |
|-----|---------|
| Tuesday | Long-form OR Short |
| Wednesday | Short |
| Thursday | Long-form OR Short |
| Friday | Short |
| Saturday | Short |
